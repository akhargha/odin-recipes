Chicken Biryani

This is a chicken dish popular in the Indian Subcontintent and beyond

This dish is cooked by adding layers of coloured and flavoured rice with chicken and cooking/baking it.

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Garam Masala
  • Cashews and Raisins
  • Biryani Masala
  • Ghee
  1. Cook Rice with some garam masala and cashews and raisins.
  2. Cook Chicken with the garam and biryani masala with the base as onions and tomatoes
  3. Make multiple layers of the rice and chicken and cook/bake
  4. Your chicken biryani is ready
